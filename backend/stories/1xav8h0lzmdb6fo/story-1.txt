Noah Lyles' bid for an Olympic sprint double was wrecked by Covid-19. Despite winning the 100m title, he struggled in the 200m final, finishing third. Lyles kept his positive test a secret, but admitted the virus affected his performance. He experienced shortness of breath and chest pain but was proud to win bronze. Lyles had hoped to compete in the 4x100m and 4x400m relays but announced his Olympics were over. He expressed joy despite the challenges and praised his team's potential to win in the relays.
